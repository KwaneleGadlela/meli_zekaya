About Us

About Us

Meli Zekaya is a fast-growing, 100% black woman-owned IT service solutions company. Our IT company provides a comprehensive range of IT services to clients worldwide, including software quality assurance, software quality engineering, accessibility to a resource pool for IT service provision, software testing as a service, training, IT service optimization, and process optimization.

We are committed to delivering high-quality services that meet our clients' needs and exceed their expectations.

At Meli Zekaya, we are dedicated to delivering exceptional customer experiences, enhancing operational efficiency, reducing costs, and increasing productivity for our clients.We are committed to remaining at the forefront of the industry, and we continuously innovate and improve our processes to exceed customer expectations.

Our customized and client-centric approach distinguishes us from other IT service providers as we tailor solutions to meet the unique needs of each organization we work with. We are passionate about creating innovative and breakthrough technological solutions for businesses of all sizes.

Our team of highly skilled professionals is devoted to helping our clients achieve their goals by providing cutting-edge technology services at an affordable price.
