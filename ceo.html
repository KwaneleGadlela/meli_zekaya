About Our CEO


  • Adetola is a thought leader and visionary professional who serves on numerous executive and non-executive boards and has built a remarkable career spanning various domains, including software quality assurance, IT project management, test management, program executive roles, and IT operations. Her extensive experience and expertise are invaluable assets to our organization. An award-winning poet and speaker with numerous awards and recognitions, she holds the record for the 2019 and 2023 top three outstanding Nigerian female achievements in South Africa, amongst many others.

    Adetola's exceptional leadership skills were acknowledged by several reputable organizations, such as Nedbank, Vodacom, Assupol, and the Federal Ministry of Tourism in Nigeria, which entrusted her with test management responsibilities. In this role, she established robust testing processes, implemented effective quality assurance strategies, and managed a team of proficient testers. Her relentless pursuit of quality and her ability to optimize testing efforts resulted in improved software quality and reduced time-to-market for projects she managed from inception to completion.

    Currently, Adetola Bakinson wears multiple hats as the head of business units and CEO in various organizations, playing a pivotal role in overseeing overall operations. Leveraging her diverse background and extensive experience in different IT domains, she provides strategic guidance and drives operational excellence. Her commitment to fostering a culture of innovation, collaboration, and continuous improvement empowers our team to deliver exceptional products and services to our clients.

    Adetola Bakinson is highly esteemed for her visionary leadership, unwavering dedication to excellence, and her ability to drive organizational growth. Her unique blend of technical expertise, project management skills, and operational acumen positions her as a driving force in leading our company towards continued success in an ever-evolving industry. With her strategic vision and steadfast commitment to excellence, we are confident in our ability to thrive and consistently deliver exceptional value to our clients.
